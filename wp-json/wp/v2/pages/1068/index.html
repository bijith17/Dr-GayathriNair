My Bio<\/h2><\/div><\/div><\/div>\n\n\n\n

I’m a dedicated dental professional committed to excellence in patient care and continuous learning. With experience in various prestigious institutions, I’ve managed outpatients, performed surgeries, and served as a Senior Lecturer. I’m registered with the Kerala Dental Council and am a proud member of esteemed organizations like AOMSI and AOCMF. Additionally, I was honored to receive the Best Speaker Award in the Clinical Club, highlighting my dedication to excellence in dentistry.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
